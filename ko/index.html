Kang Eun Jeon

Kang Eun Jeon is a post-doctoral researcher in the Convergence Research Institute at SungKyunKwan University (SKKU). Before joining SKKU, he received his Ph.D. degree in Electronic and Computer Engineering under the supervision of Prof. James She. His doctoral thesis focused on designing smart BLE beacons to enable sustainable IoT via hardware, firmware, and machine learning co-designs. His research interests now expand to various on-device machine learning techniques that jointly optimize device power consumption and performance.
